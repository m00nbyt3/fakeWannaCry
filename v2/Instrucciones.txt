1. Agregar script y html

Stop-Process -Name 'chrome' -Force;
Start-Process 'taskkill.exe' -ArgumentList '-F -IM explorer.exe';

$fullPath = Resolve-Path .\index.html
Start-Process 'chrome.exe' $fullPath;



2.Crear acceso directo con lo siguiente:

C:\Windows\System32\WindowsPowerShell\v1.0\powershell.exe -NoProfile -Command ".\script.ps1"



3.Ocultar script y html

attrib +s +h script.ps1
attrib +s +h index.html



4.Para desocultarlos utilizar

attrib -s -h script.ps1
attrib -s -h index.html
